# ROTRA Arena: Futuristic Badminton Court Background Design Spec

This specification describes the high-tech, branded environment shown in the reference image. It is designed to be used as a background for character renders or gameplay concepts within the ROTRA brand universe.

## 1. Overall Atmosphere & Theme
*   **Vibe:** Futuristic, high-energy, premium sports facility, "Cyberpunk Athletics."
*   **Lighting Mood:** Dark and moody interior contrasted by aggressive, bright neon accents.
*   **Key Elements:** Circuitry patterns, geometric shapes (hexagons), reflective surfaces, and integrated branding.

## 2. Color Palette & Materials
*   **Primary Surfaces:** Dark brushed metal panels, raw polished concrete floors, glass railings. The environment is predominantly dark grey and black.
*   **Accent Lighting (Branding Colors):**
    *   **Electric Green (`#00FF88`):** Used for primary court lines, logos, and main circuit paths.
    *   **Deep Violet/Purple:** Used for secondary accent lighting, court borders, and the net header tape.
*   **Overhead Lighting:** Bright, cool white light from massive fixtures.

## 3. Architectural Features
*   **Structure:** A large, double-height industrial-futuristic arena.
*   **Mezzanine:** Elevated observation decks/balconies run along both lateral sides of the court, featuring glass railings and integrated neon circuit patterns on the underside and walls.
*   **Ceiling:** High industrial ceiling with exposed structure, dominated by two massive, glowing hexagonal LED ring fixtures that provide the main downward illumination.
*   **Windows:** The far end wall features large, angular panoramic windows looking out onto a futuristic cityscape.

## 4. The Court (Centerpiece)
*   **Flooring:** Highly polished, dark concrete that acts as a mirror, reflecting the neon lights above and around it.
*   **Court Markings:** Regulation badminton court lines rendered as glowing neon light strips (green for inner lines, purple for outer boundary).
*   **The Net:** A futuristic "energy net." The posts are glowing green structures, and the net mesh itself is a translucent energy field capped with a glowing purple top band.
*   **Center Branding:** A large, glowing green ROTRA orbit logo is integrated into the center of the court floor.

## 5. Integrated Branding Elements
*   **Sideline LED Boards:** Low-profile digital display barriers run along the sides of the court. They glow with green light and continuously display:
    *   The circular ROTRA logo.
    *   The wordmark "ROTRA" (bold, all-caps, geometric sans-serif).
    *   The tagline "RUN THE GAME."
*   **Wall Circuits:** The metal walls are covered in integrated glowing green and purple patterns resembling printed circuit boards (PCBs).

## 6. Background Environment (Outside)
*   **View:** Through the large rear windows, a dense, futuristic metropolis is visible at dusk/night.
*   **City Aesthetics:** Towering skyscrapers with many neon signs and flying vehicle traffic. The atmospheric lighting outside is dominated by hazy pinks, purples, and blues, complementing the interior lighting.
