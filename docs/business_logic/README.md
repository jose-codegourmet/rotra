# Badminton App — Business Logic Index

This platform is split into **three separate applications**, each with a distinct audience, scope, and interface. All three share a common backend but are built and deployed independently.

---

## The Three Apps

### 1. Client App — `client_app/`
The primary user-facing product. Players join clubs, attend queue sessions, view their stats, and interact with their badminton community. Club Owners manage their clubs and members. Que Masters run sessions from setup to close.

**Primary users**: Players, Club Owners, Que Masters
**Scope**: Everything a badminton player or club operator needs

→ [Client App README](./client_app/README.md)

---

### 2. Admin App — `admin_app/`
An internal operations dashboard for platform management. Contains kill switches to toggle features on/off without a deploy, environment controls (staging vs production), Club Owner application approvals, platform moderation tools, global config for gamification and skill dimensions, and platform-wide analytics.

**Primary users**: Platform admins (internal team)
**Scope**: Platform control, safety, and configuration

→ [Admin App README](./admin_app/README.md)

---

### 3. Umpire App — `umpire_app/`
A minimal, focused scoring interface. Opened via a one-time token/QR generated by the Que Master. The umpire scores a single assigned match — tapping points, undoing, and submitting the final score. Scores are broadcast in real-time back to the Que Master and all session participants. No login required.

**Primary users**: Anyone assigned as Quick Umpire (authenticated or guest)
**Scope**: Live scoring of a single match; nothing else

→ [Umpire App README](./umpire_app/README.md)
